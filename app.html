📊 Growth Strategy Guide

🔥 High Growth (Maximum Engagement)

Aggressive posting for rapid audience building
Instagram: 2-3 Posts/day + 5-7 Stories/day
TikTok: 3-5 Videos/day + 1 LIVE/day
X/Twitter: 8-12 Tweets/day
Snapchat: 5-8 Stories/day
OnlyFans: 2-3 Posts/day + 3-5 Stories/day + 2-3 Lives/week
Fansly: 2-3 Posts/day + 3-5 Stories/day + 2-3 Lives/week
Reddit: 2-3 Posts/day (different subreddits)
LinkedIn: 1-2 Posts/day
YouTube: 1 Video/day or 5-7/week
Pinterest: 10-15 Pins/day
Threads: 5-8 Posts/day

⚡ Medium Growth (Sustainable)

Consistent posting for steady audience growth
Instagram: 1 Post/day + 3-4 Stories/day
TikTok: 2-3 Videos/day + 3-4 Lives/week
X/Twitter: 5-7 Tweets/day
Snapchat: 3-5 Stories/day
OnlyFans: 1-2 Posts/day + 2-3 Stories/day + 1-2 Lives/week
Fansly: 1-2 Posts/day + 2-3 Stories/day + 1-2 Lives/week
Reddit: 1-2 Posts/day
LinkedIn: 1 Post/day or 5/week
YouTube: 3-4 Videos/week
Pinterest: 5-8 Pins/day
Threads: 3-5 Posts/day

💼 Low Growth (Maintenance)

Minimum posting to maintain audience
Instagram: 1 Post/day + 2-3 Stories/day
TikTok: 1-2 Videos/day + 1-2 Lives/week
X/Twitter: 3-5 Tweets/day
Snapchat: 2-3 Stories/day
OnlyFans: 1 Post/day + 1-2 Stories/day + 1 Live/week
Fansly: 1 Post/day + 1-2 Stories/day + 1 Live/week
Reddit: 1 Post/day
LinkedIn: 3-4 Posts/week
YouTube: 1-2 Videos/week
Pinterest: 3-5 Pins/day
Threads: 2-3 Posts/day

💡 Pro Tips

  • Consistency matters more than volume
  • Post during peak hours for your audience (usually 9am-12pm, 6pm-9pm EST)
  • Premium platforms (OF/Fansly) need 1-2 lives per week minimum
  • Stories disappear in 24hrs - post more liberally
  • Quality > Quantity - one great post beats three mediocre ones
