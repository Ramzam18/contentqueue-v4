Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Content Queue collects the following information:

  • Email address (for account authentication)
  • Mobile phone number (optional, only if you enable SMS notifications)
  • Content scheduling data (platforms, post types, and times you create)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide our content scheduling service:

  • Email: Account login and password recovery
  • Phone Number: Send SMS reminders for your scheduled content (only if you opt-in)
  • Schedule Data: Display your content calendar and send timely reminders

3. SMS Notifications

If you enable SMS notifications:

  • We send reminders 30 minutes before your scheduled tasks
  • Optional morning digest at 7am with your daily overview
  • Message frequency: 1-5 messages per day, depending on your schedule
  • Standard message and data rates may apply

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Share your information with third parties
  • Sell your data
  • Use your information for marketing purposes
  • Send promotional or advertising messages

We only share data with:

  • Twilio (SMS delivery service) - only your phone number to send reminders
  • Stripe (payment processing) - only billing information for subscriptions

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your information. Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access, update, or delete your information anytime in account settings
  • Opt out of SMS notifications by unchecking "Enable SMS Reminders" or replying STOP to any message
  • Delete your account and all associated data
